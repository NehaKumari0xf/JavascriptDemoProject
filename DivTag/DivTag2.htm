Web Development is of 2 types basically: Front-End & Back-End.

Front-End contains various technologies/languages/libraries for developing the UI part of your website.

Here, you'll learn:

  • HTML: for writing the code of your website. It's like a skeleton of your website.
  • CSS: for styling and designing your website.
  • Bootstrap: It's a framework that helps you design your website easily.
  • Javascript: for making your website functional.
  • Jquery: for writing JS easily.
  • React: It's a javascript library for making amazing UI.

Back-End contains storing your data & working with databases. Here you'll learn NodeJS, Django, etc.
